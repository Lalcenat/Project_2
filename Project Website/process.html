Proposal:

The premise of our project was to search for relationships between the life expectancy of a country and its GDP over time.

Data Extraction Sources:

  • GDP: Kaggle CSV
  • Life Expectancy: World Bank Database

Data Transformation:

  • We loaded the CSV’s into pandas data frames.
  • We cleaned the data frames, renamed the countries to make sure they matched in both datasets, and dropped the records that contained null values.
  • We loaded the clean data into an SQL database.
  • We plotted the data to show the relationships between GDP and life expectancy.

Challenges

Our main challenge was finding a good source for our data. Our initial topic required us to scrape data from glassdoor.com in order to find reviews for fortune 500 companies. This proved to be challenging, because the overall reviews were formatted in a complex visual format. We also tried an API, but it was a paid service and required approval from glassdoor.com. Considering these issues, we decided to change the entire scope of our project. In conclusion, we used CSV’s from Kaggle and the World Bank website.
